  ABOUT US

OUR VISION AND MISSION

Making healthcare easy by utilizing technology, healthcare partners, and services to make healthcare more Accessible, Affordable and Accountable resulting in better outcome from existing healthcare resources and infrastructure.

  OUR PROJECTS

Health Camps

Our Health camps are setup with the goal of early detection, creating awareness and providing a support system for the people in rural areas. We provide BP and Sugar check, doctor consultation, awareness around health and hygiene, explaining government schemes available for better healthcare. Every family is informed of the medical condition, any next steps if needed and access to our 24x7 healthcare helpline.

School Health

We beleive Healthcare and healthy lifestyle starts early. If the child is aware so will be the family. Our School Healh Program is focused on partnering with schools to facilitate early checkup ( Eye, Dental, Hearing, General Checkup), create awareness such as hygiene, healthy eating, discourage use of tobacco, alcohol etc.

24x7 Healthcare helpline for all

Lack of information and a relaible / easy source of information continues to be a big challenge in making healthcare accessible to the underprivileged. In a lot of cases people are unaware of the numerous government healtthcare programs and facilities which remain underutilized. We at healthok are committed to providing a 24x7 helpline where anyone can call for any kind of support related to heathcare.

Free consult with clinical specialists

Hand holding is what everyone needs when it comes to healthcare. In lot of cases just a quick access to a qualified healthcare professional can mean the difference between taking adequate and proper treatment vs gettinng no care at all. Coupled with our 24x7 helpline is support of qualified healthcare professionals that can provide anywhere from a ful fledged telemedicine call with a doctor to quick help needed from a qualified healthcare professional.
