OUR VISION AND MISSION

To help the Government in its policies to see a Disease free india. To identify the needy who requires Medical & Educational Aid and assist her/him to become a respectable Citizen and see his purpose of his birth.

School Health

The School Health Programme is the only public sector programme specifically focused on school age children. Its focus is to address holistically the health and nutrition needs of children in a manner which fulfils the needs of today's lifestyle, hence activities like yoga and counselling facilities have been incorporated.
